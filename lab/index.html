Research Projects

Velocity tracking for super-resolution ultrasound imaging

Evaluate tracking and velocity estimation methods for super-resolution.

Develop an ultrasound sensing technique for detection of metallic markers.

Motion Correction for 3D Super-resolution Ultrasound Imaging

Implement motion correction that can work on 3D ultrasound images with speckle.

Translation of Super-resolution Ultrasound Imaging to the Clinic

Ultrasonic bone characterization

Develop a novel ultrasonic bone thickness and mineral density measurement technique.

Design and develop an ultrasound imaging technique in air for an application you want.
