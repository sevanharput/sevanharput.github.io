New Ultrasound Bone Quantification Project

Tue, 01 Jun 2021
Sevan Harput – “A New Non-Invasive Technique for Bone Porosity Imaging”
Event: BioMedical Imaging at LSBU: A Vision of the Future in Imaging Technology
Organiser: London South Bank University
Date & Time: June 8, 2021 (10:20)


Join us to take the next step with your business and learn about the cutting edge developments in the area of BioMedical Imaging and Image Processing. The speakers will discuss successes in research, collaboration and commercialisation – in the areas of Ultrasound, Microwave Ultrawide Band Imaging, Infrared sensing and applications of machine vision and deep learning all to increase the quality of patient outcomes.


Sevan Harput
Associate Professor in Electrical and Electronic Engineering

Sevan is a Associate Professor in the at Division of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, London South Bank University, where he leads the SPEED Ultrasound Lab. The ultrasound researchlab aims to develop new imaging and sensing technologies using acoustic waves at ultrasonic frequencies.
